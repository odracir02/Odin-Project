Paella


Description:

Paella is a traditional Spanish dish that originated in Valencia. It is a rice dish typically cooked in a wide, shallow pan with saffron, vegetables, and a variety of meats such as chicken, rabbit, or seafood. The dish gets its distinctive golden color and fragrant aroma from the saffron used in the cooking process. The ingredients are layered and cooked together to create a delicious, savory and flavorful dish that is perfect for sharing with friends and family. Paella is a popular dish in Spain and is enjoyed by people all over the world.


Ingredients:

  • Rice (short-grain)
  • Saffron threads
  • Olive oil
  • Crushed tomato
  • Green beans
  • Artichokes
  • Chicken
  • Rabbit
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil and introduce the meat and vegetables until browned.

  2. Add the tomato and fry it, when it is ready add the paprika.

  3. Fill the paddle halfway with water.

  4. Bring to a boil and add the rice making a line in the center until it comes out of the water.

  5. Spread well and boil until all the water evaporates.
