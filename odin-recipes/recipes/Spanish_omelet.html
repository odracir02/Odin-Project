Spanish omelet


Description:

A potato omelet is a traditional Spanish dish made with sliced potatoes, onions, eggs, and olive oil. The potatoes are cooked in the oil until tender, then mixed with beaten eggs and cooked until firm.


Ingredients:

  • 2-3 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • 4-6 eggs
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a large non-stick skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.

  2. Add the sliced potatoes and onions to the skillet and season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the potatoes are tender and lightly browned.

  3. While the potatoes are cooking, beat the eggs in a mixing bowl and season with salt and pepper.

  4. Once the potatoes are cooked, use a slotted spoon to remove them from the skillet and add them to the beaten eggs. Mix well.

  5. Drain most of the olive oil from the skillet, leaving just a thin layer.

  6. Pour the egg and potato mixture into the skillet and cook over low-medium heat until the bottom is set.

  7. Using a plate flip the omelet

  8. Leave it on the fire until you think the other side is done and plate it.
